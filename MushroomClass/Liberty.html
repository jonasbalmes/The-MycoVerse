Liberty Cap

Psilocybe semilanceata

Description

Psilocybe semilanceata, more commonly known as the Liberty cap, is a psychoactive type of mushroom and is one of the most widely distributed psilocybin mushrooms in nature, and is also one of the most potent.

This mushroom has a distinctive conical bell-shaped cap, with a small nipple-like protrusion at the top. They are yellow to brown in gradient and are found in grassland habitats, especially within wetter areas. This mushroom feeds off of and grows on decaying matter more specifically, decaying grass roots. It is widely distributed in temperate areas of the Northern Hemishpere, particularly in Europe.

Psychoactive Effect

This mushroom is one of the most widely distributed psilocybin mushrooms in nature, and is one of the most potent. This mushroom inccurs hallucinogenic properties with its effects being as potent as a mild dose of LSD and can vary depending on the mood, situation and expectation of the user. The effect can last for about half an hour, but could reach 9 hours depending on how much were eaten.
